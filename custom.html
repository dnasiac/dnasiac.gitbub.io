Custom Requests

Chosen 1 Creations is here for all your customized needs! If you are interested in purchasing a custom shirt, please fill out the information with as much detail as possible. If you have a design, please send it to the Chosen 1 Creations email address.

General Tips for Custom Orders

  • Inlcude details about design such as colors, size, and placement. If you cannot describe this in words, send an email.
  • Include vinyl type (solid, glitter, bling)
  • Include shirt type, such as v-neck, hoodie, etc.
  • Customization is subject to owner pricing and may not reflect pricing of other items on website
  • Customization is not limited to shirts. We can customize totes, glasses, and more!
